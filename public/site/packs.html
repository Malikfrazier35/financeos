FAQ

Common questions

Do I need to be on the Business plan to buy a pack?
Yes. Pro packs are role-based add-ons that build on top of the Business plan's foundation — workspace, live GL data, AI copilot, and admin console. If you're on Starter or Growth, upgrade to Business first, then add the packs that match your team's roles.
Can I buy more than one pack?
Absolutely. Many teams pair Controller (for close + accuracy) with FP&A (for forecasting + scenarios), or CFO with CEO for executive operating cadence. Each pack bills independently and can be added or removed without affecting the others.
Is the pack per-seat or per-organization?
Per-organization. One purchase unlocks the pack for everyone in your org with the appropriate role permission. There's no separate per-user fee on top of the $999/mo.
What happens if I cancel?
You retain access through the end of the current billing period, then the role-specific surfaces are gracefully hidden — your underlying GL data, forecasts, and Castford foundation are unaffected. Re-subscribe at any time and your saved configurations come right back.
Are taxes included?
Stripe Tax automatically calculates VAT, GST, or US sales tax based on your billing address at checkout. The price shown is exclusive of tax; the final amount on your invoice will reflect the rate for your jurisdiction.
