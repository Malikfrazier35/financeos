Problems we solve

Finance teams face the same bottlenecks every month. Castford was built to eliminate them.

Slow monthly close

Manual reconciliations and spreadsheet hand-offs stretch the close to 10+ days. Castford automates journal entries, variance flagging, and consolidation so teams can close in days, not weeks.

Disconnected planning

Budgets live in spreadsheets, actuals live in the ERP, and headcount lives in HR. Castford unifies revenue, expense, and workforce planning on a single platform with live data connections.

Reporting bottlenecks

Board decks and management reports take days to assemble. Castford generates audit-ready reports and dashboards that update automatically as numbers change.

Spreadsheet risk

Version control issues, broken formulas, and no audit trail put the business at risk. Castford provides full version history, role-based access, and SOX-ready controls out of the box.

Expensive legacy tools

Traditional EPM platforms require lengthy implementations and six-figure annual contracts. Castford deploys in days and scales with your team at a fraction of the cost.

No AI leverage

Most finance tools still require manual analysis. Castford includes an AI copilot that explains variances, surfaces anomalies, and answers natural-language questions about your data.
