Under the Hood

How LiftOff™ actually works

Not a lift-and-shift copy job. LiftOff uses AI to understand the intent behind your models and rebuild them natively for the Castford Engine.

Semantic Model Parser

Builds an Abstract Syntax Tree from your source platform — every formula, every list relationship, every module dependency. Understands nested IF logic, LOOKUP chains, and cross-dimensional references.

100%
Formula coverage

Formula Transpiler

Converts proprietary calculation syntax into native Castford formulas. Breaks complex nested statements into optimized, readable building blocks — improving performance and maintainability.

94%
Auto-converted, no manual rewrite

Dimension Mapper

Automatically maps hierarchies, list structures, and dimension metadata. Detects orphaned items, circular references, and inconsistent granularity before they cause downstream errors.

200+
Data quality checks

Variance Validator

Runs source vs. target comparison on every calculation output. Flags variances above configurable thresholds and generates a full reconciliation report for sign-off.

<0.01%
Accepted variance threshold

Schema Guardian

Validates every table structure, column mapping, and data type conversion. Ensures referential integrity across multi-entity structures and prevents data loss during transfer.

Your Timeline

Two weeks from kickoff to live

Here's what a typical LiftOff engagement looks like, from discovery call to production cutover.

Day 1–2
Discovery

Platform Scan & Data Audit

LiftOff connects to your source environment via read-only API. We map every model, module, list, formula, user permission, and data dependency. You receive a Migration Blueprint — a full inventory of what will be converted, optimized, or flagged for review.

Day 3–5
Convert

AI Formula Translation & Schema Mapping

The transpiler converts proprietary formulas into native Castford syntax. Dimension hierarchies, list relationships, and time ranges are rebuilt. Complex nested logic is refactored into cleaner, faster building blocks. Historical data is streamed into the Castford Engine.

Day 6–9
Validate

Side-by-Side Verification

Every calculation output is compared against the source platform. The Variance Validator flags any discrepancy above 0.01%. Your team reviews a reconciliation dashboard and signs off on accuracy before cutover.

Day 10–12
Train

Team Onboarding & Parallel Run

Self-paced video training plus live sessions tailored to your workflows. Both platforms run in parallel so your team can verify outputs in their real planning cycle before committing to the switch.

Day 14
Go Live

Production Cutover & CSM Assignment

Flip the switch. A dedicated Customer Success Manager is assigned for the first 90 days. 99.9% uptime SLA from day one. Your legacy platform contract? We'll help you negotiate the exit.
