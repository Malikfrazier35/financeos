FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know about committed spend.

Can I upgrade mid-commitment?

Yes. You can upgrade your tier at any time during your commitment. The discount percentage stays the same, and you simply pay the difference for the higher tier. For example, upgrading from Starter to Growth on a 3-year commit keeps your 35% discount and adjusts the monthly rate accordingly.

What happens if I need to downgrade?

Downgrades are handled on a case-by-case basis. If your team's needs change, reach out to your Customer Success Manager. We'll work with you to find a solution, whether that's adjusting your tier, pausing features, or restructuring your commitment.

Is the discount applied to add-ons and overages?

Committed spend discounts apply to your base subscription tier. Add-ons like additional API calls, premium integrations, or extra storage are billed separately. However, volume discount perks may apply to add-ons — talk to Sales for a custom quote.

How does the money-back guarantee work with commitments?

Annual and multi-year committed spend customers get an extended 60-day money-back guarantee (vs the standard 30 days). If you're not satisfied within the first 60 days, we'll refund you in full — no questions asked. After the MBG period, the commitment term applies as agreed.

Can I pay upfront for an additional discount?

Yes. Prepaying for the full commitment term upfront unlocks an additional 5% discount on top of the committed spend rate. For example, a 3-year Growth prepay would save you over $40,000 total. Contact Sales for prepayment terms.
