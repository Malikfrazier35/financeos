How FlowSync™ works

Not a batch sync tool. FlowSync uses change data capture and real-time streaming to keep your financial data current to the second.

Change Data Capture

Detects changes at the record level in your source systems. Only syncs what changed — no full table scans, no stale snapshots.

<30s
Change-to-sync latency

Auto-Schema Detection

Reads your source schema, maps fields to Castford dimensions, and handles type conversions automatically. No manual column mapping.

Zero
Manual mapping required

Bi-Directional Sync

Write forecasts back to your ERP, push budget approvals to Salesforce, or update headcount plans in Workday. Not read-only.

2-way
Read and write back

Data Quality Monitor

Flags duplicates, missing values, format mismatches, and anomalies before they hit your models. Quarantines bad records automatically.

99.7%
Data quality score avg

Encrypted Pipeline

AES-256 encryption in transit and at rest. SOC 2 Ready. Data never touches intermediate storage — direct source-to-engine streaming.
