Common Questions

FAQ

What happens when I exceed my consumption limits?

You'll be notified at 80% and 100% usage. Overages are billed at per-unit rates: AI queries at $0.10/query, connector syncs at $0.01/sync, report exports at $0.50/export, scenario runs at $1/run. No service interruption — we never cut access mid-cycle.

Can I switch plans mid-cycle?

Yes. Upgrades take effect immediately and are prorated. Downgrades take effect at the start of your next billing cycle. No penalties for switching.

How does Castford compare to Legacy Tool A on price?

Legacy Tool A doesn't publish pricing, but Industry Reviews lists Legacy Tool C ($1,750/mo) as a comparable alternative. Our Growth plan ($2,079/mo) includes AI Copilot, consolidation, and unlimited connectors that Legacy Tool A charges extra for.

Do you offer discounts for startups or nonprofits?

Yes. Nonprofits get 50% off any plan. Startups under $5M ARR qualify for our Emerging program — 30% off Growth for the first year. Email sales@castford.com to apply.

What's included in the 30-day money-back guarantee?

Full refund, no questions asked, within the first 30 days of any paid plan. Enterprise contracts have a separate evaluation period negotiated at signing.
