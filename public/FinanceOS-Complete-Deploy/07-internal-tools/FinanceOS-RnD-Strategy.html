Building the Future of
Autonomous Finance

Our R&D strategy centers on four pillars: AI-native intelligence, connector ecosystem expansion, customer account intelligence, and predictive automation. Here's where we're investing and why.

Strategic Pillars

Four Pillars of R&D Investment

Every engineering dollar maps to one of these four pillars. Each one compounds on the others to create a platform that gets smarter with every customer.

40% of R&D

AI-Native Intelligence

Our largest investment area. We're building AI that doesn't just assist — it reasons, predicts, and acts autonomously on behalf of finance teams. This includes the AI Copilot 2.0 visible reasoning chain, natural language report generation, and anomaly detection that learns your business patterns.

LLM Fine-Tuning Reasoning Chains NLP Queries Anomaly Detection Auto-Narrative
25% of R&D

Connector Ecosystem

Data is the lifeblood of FP&A. We're expanding from 50+ connectors to 100+ by end of 2026, with a focus on real-time bi-directional sync, universal data mapping, and a public Connector SDK so customers and partners can build their own.

Bi-Directional Sync Connector SDK Auto-Mapping Webhook Events Partner Ecosystem
20% of R&D

Customer Account Intelligence

Multi-entity management, role-based access at scale, usage analytics, and customer health scoring. We're building the account infrastructure that lets Castford serve departments of 5 and enterprises of 5,000 equally well.

Multi-Entity RBAC Usage Analytics Health Scoring SSO / SCIM
15% of R&D

Predictive Automation

Forecasting that doesn't wait to be asked. Predictive cash flow, automated variance explanations, rolling forecast engines, and triggered alerts when actuals deviate from plan. The goal: finance teams that are proactive, not reactive.

Customer Account Intelligence

Smarter Account Management at Scale

Built for teams of every size — from startup FP&A analysts to enterprise finance departments with hundreds of users.

Multi-Entity Workspaces

Manage multiple subsidiaries, business units, or regions in a single Castford account. Consolidated views with drill-down into individual entities. Intercompany eliminations handled automatically.

Granular RBAC & SCIM

Role-based access control down to the cell level. SCIM provisioning for automated user lifecycle management. SSO with Google, Microsoft, Okta, and SAML 2.0. Audit logs for every action.

Usage Analytics & Health

Customer health scoring, feature adoption tracking, and usage analytics for account managers. Know which teams are getting value and which need onboarding support before they ask.

SOC 2 & Compliance

SOC 2 Type II certified, ISO 27001, GDPR compliant. Data residency options for EU and APAC. Customer data is encrypted at rest and in transit with per-tenant isolation.

White-Label & Embedding

Enterprise accounts can white-label Castford dashboards and embed them in internal portals. Custom branding, custom domains, and iframe-safe reporting for board presentations.

Consumption & Billing Engine

Real-time usage metering, committed spend tracking, overage alerts, and self-serve plan management. Customers see exactly what they're using and what they're paying for — no surprises.
