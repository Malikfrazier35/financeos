Section 01
Platform Overview

The CFO Command Center is Castford's enterprise-tier financial intelligence platform, architected for organizations with $100M+ in revenue managing multi-entity, multi-currency financial operations. Unlike self-serve dashboard products, the Command Center is delivered through a dedicated architect engagement — a named solutions architect designs, deploys, and optimizes the platform to the client's specific chart of accounts, ERP topology, and reporting cadence.

The platform integrates directly with the client's general ledger, treasury management system, ERP (NetSuite, SAP, Oracle, Sage Intacct, Microsoft Dynamics), banking APIs, and HRIS to deliver a unified financial operating system. All data is processed through Castford's reconciliation engine, which performs continuous three-way matching across source systems with sub-second latency.
