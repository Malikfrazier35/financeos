Master Services Agreement
Review the agreement terms and complete the security questionnaire to proceed. All documents are classified per your organization's data handling policy.

1. Engagement Scope Confidential

FinanceOS Inc. ("Provider") agrees to provide Client with access to the FinanceOS Command Center platform, including dashboard templates, AI-powered analytics, data connectors, and related professional services as described in the attached Statement of Work.

2. Data Handling & Security Restricted

All Client financial data processed through the Platform shall be: (a) encrypted at rest using AES-256 and in transit using TLS 1.3; (b) stored in isolated, single-tenant environments; (c) subject to SOC 2 Type II audited controls; (d) accessible only by authorized personnel with role-based access control (RBAC). Provider maintains ISO 27001 certification and undergoes annual third-party penetration testing.

3. Intellectual Property Confidential

Client retains all rights to its financial data. Dashboard configurations, custom KPIs, and branded assets created during the engagement are licensed exclusively to Client. Provider retains ownership of the underlying platform technology, algorithms, and template frameworks.

4. Term & Termination Internal

This agreement commences on the date of electronic signature and continues for an initial term of 12 months, with automatic renewal for successive 12-month periods unless either party provides 60 days written notice of non-renewal. Early termination requires 30 days notice with prorated refund of prepaid fees.

5. Limitation of Liability Internal

Provider's aggregate liability under this Agreement shall not exceed the total fees paid by Client in the 12 months preceding the claim. Neither party shall be liable for indirect, incidental, special, consequential, or punitive damages.
