Operational Playbook — How Sales Runs It Day-to-Day

CRM Pipeline — Services Stages (Parallel Track in HubSpot)

Every SaaS deal with $50K+ ACV gets a parallel "Services Opportunity" in the CRM. AEs are required to qualify services potential on every enterprise deal.

S1
Services Identified
AE flags services need during discovery. Tags skill requirements.
→
S2
SOW Drafted
AE + SE scope the engagement. Workforce Ops provides contractor availability.
→
S3
Client Review
SOW sent. AE manages pricing negotiation. Margin floor enforced by Deal Desk.
→
S4
Signed / Deploying
SOW signed via DocuSign. Workforce Ops deploys contractors within 5 days.
→
S5
Delivering
CSM takes over. Weekly check-ins. Quarterly reviews. Expansion identification.

Weekly Sales Cadence

Monday 9am — Pipeline Review VP + AEs
Tuesday 10am — Services Forecast AEs + Ops
Wednesday 2pm — Deal Desk VP + Finance
Thursday 11am — Delivery Standup CSM + Ops
Friday 3pm — Win/Loss Review All Sales

Quarterly Business Reviews (QBR)

CSM leads client QBRs with contractor team. Sales uses QBR insights to drive expansion.

Delivery scorecard presentation CSM
ROI analysis (hours saved, cost reduction) AE + CSM
Roadmap alignment + upcoming needs AE
Contractor performance review Ops
Expansion proposal (if applicable) AE
