How we build

Three principles that shape every decision in the product.

Ship, then explain

Working software before marketing. If a feature isn't live and usable, we don't talk about it. Every claim on this site maps to a real capability you can touch today.

Transparency over polish

Pre-revenue transparency beats enterprise theater. We tell you exactly what's built, what's coming, and what's still rough. No fake logos, no manufactured social proof.

Operator empathy

Every feature is evaluated against: "would a controller at close time actually want to use this?" If the answer is no, we rebuild until it is.
